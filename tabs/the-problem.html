The problem

Believe it or not, the root of the problem points at climate change as the main suspect. Australia has been in a drought from 2017 untill present. Global warming makes atmospheric circulation change, affecting Australia by decreasing rainfall in autumn and winter, making the vegatation so dry that is is technically fuel for fires, lengthing the bushfire season.

Everyone can contribute as an individual, but if countries as a whole don't start acting now, the future for Australians, and consequentely, the world, seems to be bright, but not in the positive meaning of this world. More fires will begin to spread around the continents. These could lead to more serius problems in the future.
