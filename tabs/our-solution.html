Starting Fires to Save Australia

As crazy as it may sound, the best way to stop wildfires is by having counter-fires. Think of it as a vaccine, a person goes to the doctor and gets the virus injected in them that way the virus later recorgnizes the host and it won't cause the individual to go ill. This is pretty much the same case with counter-fires. Controlled burns have long been practiced by the natives of North America and Australia. In modern times, these have been used, and sometimes they have successfully stopped the wildfires from stopping, conserving the zone's ecosystem, and sometimes even saving human lives.

Further Research must be done

Unfortunately, controlled burns have gone out of control at times, making the situation even more devastating. The reality is that we don't completely understand how to utilize this solution. Further research will need to be done before we can apply it on a mass scale.
